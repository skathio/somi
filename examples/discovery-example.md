## `decisions.md` — a crossroads, verified with the user

```markdown
## D2 — Deployment model: self-hosted-only vs. dual (self-host + managed cloud)

> Status: `accepted` • Date: 2026-06-02 • Verified with user: yes

### Decision

v1 ships **self-hosted-only** (single OCI container + Postgres), no managed-cloud offering yet.

### Alternatives considered

#### Option A — Self-hosted-only (chosen)
- Pros: directly attacks C1 (the clearest unmet need); keeps PHI on the clinic's infrastructure,
  shrinking our HIPAA blast radius; one deployment target to support in v1.
- Cons: assumption A1 (clinics will self-manage a container) is unvalidated; no recurring revenue
  from hosting.

#### Option B — Dual (self-host + managed cloud) — rejected
- Pros: serves clinics that won't self-host; recurring revenue.
- Cons: we become a HIPAA Business Associate on day one (BAA, audits, breach liability) — a large
  cost the research says we can defer; doubles the deployment surface for v1.
- Reason for rejection: the research shows self-host is the *differentiating* gap; managed cloud is
  a fast-follow once A1 is validated.

### Discovery questions
(User picked A directly after seeing the HIPAA-liability cost of B; discovery not used.)

### Consequences
- Positive: smaller compliance surface; clean differentiation.
- Negative: bounded by A1 — if clinics won't self-manage, we must add Option B sooner.
- Reversibility: moderate — managed cloud is additive; the core stays the same.
```

## What this example demonstrates

- **Research grounds everything** — every requirement traces back to a cited complaint or business
  goal in the `README.md` map; no requirement is free-floating.
- **Signal vs. noise is explicit** — the report says *how many independent sources* back each
  complaint, so the team can trust (or discount) it.
- **Crossroads are verified** — D2 shows options with concrete pros/cons (including the HIPAA-BAA
  cost of the rejected option) and the user's choice.
- **The document set adapts** — `data-privacy.md` was *added* because PHI is in scope; the decision
  and reason are recorded in `README.md`.
- **The design-depth boundary holds** — the SDD sets direction and the expensive-to-reverse call,
  then explicitly hands detailed sync design to the planner. R&D and planning don't duplicate.
- **Clean handoff** — `/plan clinic-scheduler` consumes the foundation instead of re-deriving it.
